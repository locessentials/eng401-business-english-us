# Example Financial Report: MercadoLibre, Inc.

*This is a model report showing the expected format, analysis depth, and writing style for your financial analysis assignment.*

---

## Introduction

This report analyzes the financial performance of MercadoLibre, Inc. (NASDAQ: MELI) for the six months ending June 30, 2025. MercadoLibre, Inc. is the largest e-commerce company in Latin America, operating online shopping websites and digital payment services in 18 countries including Argentina, Brazil, Mexico, and others. The company is different from other MercadoLibre entities because it is the main holding company that owns all the regional operations. This report features analysis of MercadoLibre's balance sheets and income statements from 2024 and 2025. Understanding how to read financial statements like those of MercadoLibre is important for translation professionals because we often work with international companies and need to understand their business health and performance.

## Balance Sheet Summary

The balance sheet shows what MercadoLibre owns (assets) and owes (liabilities) at specific dates. I compared the company's position on June 30, 2025 with December 31, 2024.

| Balance Sheet Item | June 30, 2025 | Dec. 31, 2024 | Change |
|-------------------|---------------|---------------|---------|
| Current Assets | $26,062 million | $20,142 million | +29.4% |
| Total Assets | $32,948 million | $25,196 million | +30.8% |
| Current Liabilities | $21,750 million | $16,603 million | +31.0% |
| Total Equity | $5,713 million | $4,351 million | +31.3% |
| **Current Ratio** | **1.20** | **1.21** | **-0.8%** |

MercadoLibre's balance sheet shows significant growth in all areas. Total assets increased by 30.8%, which means the company has grown substantially in just six months. Current assets (cash and other items that can quickly become cash) increased by 29.4%, showing the company has good liquidity. However, current liabilities (debts that must be paid soon) also increased by 31.0%. The current ratio decreased slightly from 1.21 to 1.20, but this is still above 1.0, which means the company can pay its short-term debts. The 31.3% increase in total equity shows that the company is profitable and reinvesting money to grow the business.

## Income Statement Summary

The income statement shows MercadoLibre's revenues and profits over time. I compared the six months ending June 30, 2025 with the same period in 2024.

| Income Statement Item | Six Months 2025 | Six Months 2024 | Change |
|----------------------|-----------------|-----------------|---------|
| Net Revenues | $12,725 million | $9,406 million | +35.3% |
| Gross Profit | $5,865 million | $4,389 million | +33.6% |
| Operating Income | $1,588 million | $1,254 million | +26.6% |
| Net Income | $1,017 million | $875 million | +16.2% |
| **Net Profit Margin** | **8.0%** | **9.3%** | **-1.3%** |

MercadoLibre's revenue performance was very strong, with net revenues growing 35.3% compared to the same period last year. This shows that more people are using their e-commerce platform and payment services. Gross profit increased by 33.6%, and operating income grew by 26.6%. Net income (final profit) increased by 16.2% to over $1 billion. However, the net profit margin decreased from 9.3% to 8.0%, meaning the company is making slightly less profit per dollar of sales. This could be because they are investing money in growth or facing higher costs, but the company is still very profitable overall.

## Overall Assessment

Based on my analysis, MercadoLibre's financial health appears to be strong and improving. The company is growing rapidly with 35% revenue growth and 31% asset growth, which shows successful business expansion across Latin America. The company is profitable and generating over $1 billion in net income for six months. According to recent analyst reports I found, experts are positive about MercadoLibre's performance because Latin American e-commerce is still growing rapidly, and the company is the market leader in most countries where it operates.

The slight decrease in profit margins is not concerning because it appears to be related to strategic investments in growth rather than fundamental business problems. The company's strong cash position and ability to pay its debts (current ratio above 1.0) provide financial stability for continued expansion.

## Conclusion

MercadoLibre, Inc. demonstrates strong financial performance with excellent revenue growth and solid profitability. The company's balance sheet shows successful scaling of operations with good liquidity management. While profit margins decreased slightly, this appears to be a strategic choice to invest in growth opportunities in the expanding Latin American digital economy. For translation professionals, this analysis shows how financial statements tell a story about company performance and business strategy. Understanding these documents helps us work more effectively with international business clients and translate financial content accurately.
