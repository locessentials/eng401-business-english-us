# Week 15: Business Innovation & Adaptation to Change

## Overview

This week focuses on business innovation terminology and concepts, sustainable business practices and ESG (Environmental, Social, and Governance) reporting language, and future of work and emerging business trends.

## Topics Covered

### Subtopics
- **Business innovation terminology and concepts**
- **Sustainable business practices and ESG (Environmental, Social, and Governance) reporting language**
- **Future of work and emerging business trends**

## Class Activities

### Tuesday Class Activities
- Assignment introduction: Emerging Business Trends Zine (tercer parcial)
- Introduction to concepts related to business innovation and adaptation
- ESG Activity

### Thursday Class Activities
- Discussion: Future trends in global business and implications for language professionals
- Workshop: Communicating innovative ideas across cultures

## Independent Activities

### Assignment: Emerging Business Trends Zine (tercer parcial)
- Contribute a creative piece exploring an emerging business trend, innovation, or aspect of digital transformation
- Choose from various formats: speculative business scenarios, blackout poetry from business texts, visual-text combinations, manifestos, or experimental formats
- All contributions will be compiled into a class zine showcasing diverse perspectives on the future of business
- **Due date:** Sunday of Week 15

## Learning Resources

- Examples of innovative business developments
- Distributed AI Research (DAIR) Possible Futures Zine

## Assessment

- **Emerging Business Trends Zine (tercer parcial)** (10 points)
- **Class attendance and participation** (2 points)
